# Pie Chart Template (English)

This template provides a structure for describing pie chart tasks in IELTS Writing Task 1.

## Introduction
Paraphrase the task description by stating what the pie chart(s) represent. Mention the categories and the time period if given.

## Overview
Summarise the main features shown in the chart(s). Identify the largest and smallest segments and any notable patterns or equalities. Compare charts if more than one is provided.

## Body Paragraph 1
Describe the most significant segments in detail, using specific percentages. Highlight which categories dominate and any similar proportions.

## Body Paragraph 2
Discuss the remaining segments, noting moderate or minor shares and making comparisons where relevant. Conclude with a general statement about the distribution.

> *Tip: Use comparative structures like "X accounts for twice as much as Y" and language for proportions such as "one-third", "a quarter", "approximately".*
